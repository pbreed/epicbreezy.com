Segment 1 — Done

Five flying days, twelve legs, southbound border run from Oceanside, CA to Brownsville, TX:

  • Tue 4/14 — KOKB → KCXL → E63 → E95 (Oceanside to Benson, AZ via the border)
  • Wed 4/15 — E95 → KLRU → KVHN → E38 (Arizona desert to West Texas)
  • Thu 4/16 — E38 → T89 → KOZA (through the heart of Big Bend National Park)
  • Fri 4/17 — KOZA → KUVA → KCOT (overcast cruise on top of the layer)
  • Sat 4/18 — KCOT → KBKS → KBRO (SpaceX Starbase flyby into Brownsville)

Trip totals: ~22.5 hours airborne, three video cameras (left wing, right wing, forward "Eye of God"), live-streamed via Starlink to @PaulBreed on YouTube.

Download the actual GPS track: Segment1_Trip.kml — opens in Google Earth, shows the flown path of all 12 legs with altitude profile and airport markers. ~400 KB.
